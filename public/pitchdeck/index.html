Founder
👑
Keren Alexis
Founder & Visionary · My Inner Circle Inc.

Keren brings decades of wisdom, resilience, and lived experience to every space she enters. As a licensed cosmetologist and entrepreneur, she has dedicated her life to creating meaningful connections — behind the chair, throughout her community, and now through a growing movement that empowers and inspires women everywhere.

My Why
🕊️

Loss that revealed love. When Keren lost her 4-month-old child, her friends showed up — completely, without hesitation. That moment of grace changed everything she understood about what friendship truly means.

✨

A realization at a friendship party. Surrounded by women she loved, Keren had a defining moment of clarity — that intentional gatherings have the power to transform lives and spark lasting bonds.

💛

The irreplaceable value of lifelong friendship. Through decades of genuine relationships, Keren came to know that true friendship is one of life's greatest gifts — and that every woman deserves it.

04
The Problem

Women are more connected — and more isolated — than ever.

Social media gives the illusion of community without the substance. Women across every age and background struggle to find safe spaces, genuine friendships, trusted mentors, and holistic support that honors their whole story.

58%
of women report feeling lonely at least sometimes
1 in 3
say they have no one to turn to in a crisis

No dominant multicultural women's community platform exists at scale — and mainstream apps were never built with women's wellbeing as the primary outcome.
